# Overview of Project 📕

## Group Project: Toronto Cupcake UX Website Redesign

## Website Chosen

Although I am not enrolled in ACIT 2811, I am grouped with Anna and Ava, who are taking the course. Our team selected Toronto Cupcake as the website for our UX redesign project.

!!! info "Project Context"
    This project focuses on analyzing and redesigning the Toronto Cupcake website using UX and UI principles to improve usability, navigation, and visual design.

![Toronto Cupcake Website](img-assignment01/toronto-homepage.png)

Toronto Cupcake is a well-established business that specializes in gourmet cupcakes for holidays, corporate events, weddings, and large celebrations.The company has strong brand recognition and a clear product offering. However, the current website does not reflect the quality of the brand. The design feels outdated and cluttered, making it difficult for users to navigate, find products, and complete purchases efficiently. This makes Toronto Cupcake an ideal candidate for a UX-focused redesign.

---

## Why We Chose This Topic

We chose Toronto Cupcake because it clearly demonstrates how poor UX and visual design can negatively impact user trust and engagement, even when the business itself is strong.

Specific reasons include:

- The brand is recognizable, but the website lacks polish and professionalism  
- Navigation is confusing and does not follow common usability conventions  
- The visual hierarchy is weak, making it hard to identify important information  
- The design does not align with expectations of upscale customers  
- There is a strong opportunity to improve usability without changing the core content  

This project allows us to explore how thoughtful UX and UI improvements can enhance user experience and brand perception.

!!! warning "Key UX Issue"
    Poor navigation and cluttered layout may cause users to leave the site before completing a purchase.

---

## Areas Selected for Redesign (Team Focus)

As a team, we identified several major UX and UI issues and focused on the following areas:

### Navigation Structure

![Navigation Issue](img-assignment01/navigation-problem.png)

- The current navigation is cluttered and difficult to scan  
- Important pages such as shop, cart, and ordering information are not obvious  

Our redesign focuses on a simplified, horizontal navigation menu with clear labels.

---

### Visual Hierarchy

![Visual Hierarchy Example](img-assignment01/visual-hierarchy.png)

- Headings, images, and calls to action compete for attention  
- Improvements will be made through consistent typography, spacing, and layout  

---

### Shop and Cart Experience

![Shop Page](img-assignment01/shop-experience.png)

- Current imagery does not support the “gourmet” brand positioning  
- Higher quality visuals and consistent branding will create a more premium feel  

---

### Content Organization

![Content Layout](img-assignment01/content-organization.png)

- Information is scattered and overwhelming  
- Content will be reorganized into clear, goal-oriented sections  

!!! danger "Impact on Users"
    Poor content organization and unclear structure can lead to frustration, reduced trust, and abandoned purchases.
